About Us

At Infacto Pumps India,

At Infacto, we are envisioned to serve various industries which include petrochemical plants, electrical substations, and the fine chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries.

Leveraging our world-class, advanced technology, we are committed to supplying highest quality products.

We are set to meet the growing demand for pumps in various industrial segment such as thermal and nuclear power, petrochemical and gas refineries, vegetable oil, steel, and refrigeration.

Our mission is to innovate technology and remain focused on enhancing our hydraulic technology through corporate governance and customer service.

We are committed to ensure that while we deliver products, we take into consideration the overall customer need of application, commercials, service and quality.

Our Team consists of Mechanical engineers combined with over a decade of proven experience of various industry applications and after service.
